The Six Archetypes

Which Leader Are You?

Every leader is a blend — but one archetype dominates. The assessment reveals your primary and secondary type.

♟️
The Strategist
Vision + Clarity
Sees the big picture. Makes bold, data-informed calls. May miss the human impact of decisions.
🛡️
The Shield
Loyalty + Protection
Protects the team fiercely. Absorbs pressure. Builds deep trust. May shield too much.
⚙️
The Operator
Execution + Reliability
Gets things done. Consistently delivers. Dependable. May struggle to let go and develop others.
🤝
The Connector
Empathy + Relationships
Builds deep trust. Creates safe spaces. Listens deeply. May avoid hard conversations for harmony.
🏗️
The Builder
Systems + Scale
Creates structures that outlast them. Thinks in processes. May over-systematize and lose the human element.
🔥
The Catalyst
Energy + Change
Drives transformation. Inspires action. Takes bold risks. May burn bright then fade without follow-through.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does the assessment take?
10 minutes. 12 scenarios. You reply A, B, C, or D to each. Your report arrives instantly after the last question.
Is my data confidential?
100% confidential. Your responses are encrypted and never shared with your employer, colleagues, or any third party.
Can I game the assessment?
Our scenarios have no "right" answers — every option is a valid leadership approach. The value comes from honest responses, and our contradiction detection reveals inconsistencies.
Do I need to download an app?
No. Everything happens on WhatsApp. No app, no signup, no login. Just open WhatsApp and start.
What makes this different from MBTI or DISC?
We use situational scenarios, not self-rating scales. You can't rate yourself 5/5 on delegation — instead, we present a delegation dilemma and see what you actually choose.
Is the free assessment really free?
Yes. You get your archetype, top strength, blind spot, and a coaching tip — completely free, forever. The Pro Report and coaching are optional paid upgrades.
Can I use this for my entire team?
Yes. Our enterprise plan includes team assessments, archetype distribution analysis, and a leadership dashboard. Contact us for team pricing.
How are coaches selected?
All coaches hold ICF credentials (ACC, PCC, or MCC). They're matched to your archetype and specific development needs through our proprietary matching algorithm.
