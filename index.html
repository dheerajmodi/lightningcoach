Q3/10 — THE DELEGATION DILEMMA
You have a task you could do in 2 hours, but delegating it would take 4 hours (including explaining and reviewing). Deadline is tomorrow. You:
A
Do it yourself — it's faster and quality will be better
B
Delegate anyway — the learning opportunity is worth the extra time
C
Do it yourself but schedule time next week to teach someone
D
Split it — do the critical parts, delegate the rest

Every answer maps to your leadership pattern. A = Operator. B = Builder. C = Strategist. D = Balanced. There's no wrong choice — only your truth.

Which One Are You?

Every leader is a blend, but one archetype dominates. Yours shapes how you lead, what you avoid, and where you get stuck.

♟️

The Strategist

Clear vision, big-picture thinking, decisive.

Risk: People leave feeling unseen.

🛡️

The Shield

Fiercely loyal, builds deep trust, protects.

Risk: Absorbs too much, avoids upward conflict.

⚙️

The Operator

Reliable, execution-focused, gets it done.

Risk: Can't let go. Being the best IC isn't leadership.

🤝

The Connector

Empathetic, relationship-driven, team morale.

Risk: Avoids hard decisions. Liked but not always respected.

🏗️

The Builder

Systems thinker, scales processes, creates structure.

Risk: Not everything needs a framework.

🔥

The Catalyst

Inspires change, high energy, bold moves.

Risk: Burns hot then fades. Inspiration without systems = chaos.
